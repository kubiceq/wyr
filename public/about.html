My name is Kubqo and I created this website for you.

I'm a technology student from Slovakia and I found myself stuck at home during corona pandemic. Trying to replicate the world before the covid hit, my wife-to-be created a small discord channel for us and our friends. There, we could watch movies, play games and drink beer together.

We love to play board games together, but there is such a small number of them that we could play online!

With a lot of free time and a never ending lockdown, I set out to create a simple website where me and my friends could play silly card games together.

After a few sleepless nights and a bottle of rum... Tadaa! Here is the result! I hope that this personal project will bring a little joy to your life in these daring times.
